Privacy Policy for LaVivaBot

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for LaVivaBot. This document outlines how we handle your data. By using LaVivaBot, you consent to the collection and processing of your messages as described herein.

Data Collection and Storage

LaVivaBot stores user messages indefinitely to enhance its functionality and improve user experience. If you wish to have your data (messages) removed, please contact the bot owner via Discord at 'maracujacraft'. We will address your request within one month, and may extend this period if necessary, notifying you accordingly.

Data Access

Access to your data is limited to the bot owner and one trusted collaborator responsible for bot development. We do not share your information with third parties unless illegal activity is detected, in which case we may disclose your data to law enforcement authorities as mandated by applicable law.

Data Protection Measures

We implement industry-standard security protocols to safeguard your data from unauthorized access. However, no system can guarantee complete security against potential threats.

Data Retention Policy

Your messages will remain stored indefinitely unless a removal request is made. Upon receipt of such a request, we will strive to delete your data promptly, but please allow up to one month for processing.

User Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you are entitled to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your data at any time. Should you have any queries or wish to exercise your rights, please reach out to us on Discord.

Legal Compliance

If we detect any illegal activities, we reserve the right to share your data with relevant legal authorities as required by law.
